Welcome to the Shajan

about

Mr Ali has been in the Curry business since 1980 and established Shajan Indian Restaurant in 1992. It started off as a small 85 seater set in the picturesque Ribble Valley. Due to its increased popularity the restaurant had to be extended three times to its current 205 capacity excluding the lounge. It has been nominated and won the BCA best indian restaurant in the north west twice in a row.

The Shajan motto is not to compromise on quality, service and value for money.

AWARD WINNING FOOD
